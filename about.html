who is she?

artistic
influence

As a half-asian kid of the 90s, my creative journey kicked off early on – growing up with Sailor Moon and other Manga/Anime, I was absorbing a world of vibrant colours and endless inspiration.

Until my late teens, my passion for drawing manga fueled my artistic growth. Inspired by diverse cultures and artistic genres over time, my imagination flourished and a greater interest in classical, modern and digital art soon began to grow.

In my 20s, friends and I formed an artist collective. We teamed up with promoters and clubs, and combined philosophical, interactive art exhibitions with clubbing.


mission

My goal is to revolutionise creative collaboration by crafting a tailored platform for creatives. From personal experiences, I understand that creatives with diverse expertise need each other to bring their passion projects to life. Inspired by this, my platform will serve as a matchmaker, connecting like-minded individuals for projects. Users can publish projects and find qualified creatives, streamlining collaboration and crowd-funding.
